More Than An Afterschool Program

Research findings consistently showed that Heads Up functions as a community-centered space where students feel supported, included, safe, and encouraged to grow academically, socially, and emotionally.

The website design reflects direct feedback gathered through interviews, observations, surveys, and discussions with researchers, volunteers, parents, and community members.
