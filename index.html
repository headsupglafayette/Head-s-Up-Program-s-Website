About Heads Up

A Program Built Around People, Growth, and Belonging

Who We Are

Heads Up is a community-centered afterschool program focused on supporting students academically, socially, emotionally, and personally through mentorship, collaboration, and engaging learning experiences.

Research and community feedback consistently described the program as supportive, safe, welcoming, encouraging, and fun. This website was intentionally designed to reflect those same feelings through its layout, visuals, storytelling, organization, and accessibility.

Mission

To create meaningful learning experiences and strong community connections while supporting children, families, volunteers, and researchers through collaboration and care.

What Makes This Program Special?

  • Hands-on learning
  • Supportive mentorship
  • Community engagement
  • Collaborative learning
  • Safe and welcoming environment
  • Real-world impact
